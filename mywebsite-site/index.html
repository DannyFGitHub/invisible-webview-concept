About Dinosaurs

Dinosaurs were a diverse group of reptiles that lived millions of years ago. They came in various sizes and shapes, from the massive T-Rex to the gentle giants like Brachiosaurus.

Types of Dinosaurs

There were many different types of dinosaurs, such as:

  • Tyrannosaurus Rex
  • Velociraptor
  • Triceratops
  • Stegosaurus

Fossils and Discoveries

Fossils of dinosaurs have been found all around the world, giving us valuable insights into their lives and habits.
